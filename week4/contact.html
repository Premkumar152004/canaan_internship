Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for an internship?

Fill out the contact form above selecting "Internship Inquiry" as the subject, or send us an email directly at info@canaanedtech.com with your details and resume.

What is the duration of training programs?

Training programs vary from 2-7 months depending on the domain. Each program is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and hands-on experience.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes! All participants receive completion certificates. Top performers also receive letters of recommendation that can boost your job applications.

Are Level 2 internships guaranteed?

Level 2 internships are merit-based. Students who perform exceptionally well in Level 1 internships are eligible to advance to paid Level 2 positions.

What are the prerequisites?

Basic computer knowledge and enthusiasm to learn are the main requirements. Some advanced programs may require prior programming knowledge.

Do you offer job placement?

Yes! We provide job placement assistance, resume building, interview preparation, and connect you with our partner companies for opportunities.
